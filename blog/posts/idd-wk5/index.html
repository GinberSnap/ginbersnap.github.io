IDD (Wk5): Learning Activities

04-19-25

Learning Activities

Activity 1:
Presentation will explain ethical hacking, focusing on OSINT (Open source intelligent) basic knowledge, principles, and ethical responsibilities. The presentation includes multimedia to help students understand key concepts through visual and interactive elements, and support students in building a strong foundational knowledge of ethical hacking. This activity will support the learning objective 1.

Activity 2:
Students will explore a case study and engage in a group discussion centered on legal and ethical responsibilities. Working collaboratively in small groups, they will create a set of principles forming a “Cyber Ethics Code.” This activity will support the learning objective 2.

Activity 3:
Students will be introduced to the basics of ethical hacking through a step-by-step guide. Key methods like reconnaissance, scanning, and OSINT (Open source intelligent) will be explained through a presentation using a real-life case. This activity supports learning objective 3.

Activity 4:
Students will take part in a hands-on cryptography activity. Using a free online tool, they will examine a photo and collect data using OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) methods. This exercise supports learning objective 4.
